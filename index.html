Skills and Services

I bring different music influences while planing with you the perfect music for your event. I have performed in different events, from background music to center-stage cocktail hour, and wedding ceremonies. Here is an overview of my skills and services.

Violin/ Piano Instruction

I have taught students from age 6 to age 25. I understand individual students needs and create lessons that cater to them.

Music Orchestration

I have added multiple instrument orchestration for songs of genres that range from classical to rock. If you are working on personal project, let us connect.

Online Instructions

Teaching over video chat sessions has uniques set of challenges. I have met these challenges and continued delivering excellent instructions.

Musical Evenings

Whether it is engagement proposal, graduation ceremony, a friendly luncheons or a casual party, I bring music that sets the mood.

Personal moments

Weddings, Anniversary Celebrations, and Memorial Services/Funerals are personal and require lots of thought and care. I always work with the close family memebers and create an environment that commemorate the occassion appropriately.

Professional Events

I work with corporates or individuals to bring music to their professional events. I have performed at Art Exhibits/Open Galleries, Restaurant Openings, Tech Company Events, and Fashion Shows.

Classical Performance

Over the years I have performed classical music as soloist throught the world including Greece, Germany, Italy and US.

Orchestra Performances

I have been part of several orchestras and performed at several big auditoriums. The list of orchestra performaces is a long one. Contact me if you want to learn more.

Language Competency

I am a native Greek and Albanina speaker, proficient in English and have intermediate knowledge of German, Italian, and French.
